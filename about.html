Our Story

Explain how your project started.

Specific

Fun start: play a feelings card game or roleplay.
Main idea: talk about feelings and ways to feel better.

Measurable

- Ask kids: “How do you feel?” before and after the talk.
- Quick feedback: “Did you enjoy the games and learn something?”

Achievable

-Be kind, listen, and use simple words.
- Games for little kids (4–12) to show emotions.
- Activities for older kids (12+) to share thoughts and feelings.

Relevant

- Learn about feelings and how to handle them.
- Everyone gets a chance to be heard.
- Fun crafts and games help kids feel safe to share.
- Practice ways to feel calm and understand themselves.

Time-bound

- First session: 2 months from now.
- One session per month.
- Held at the community center.
- Fully finished by end of June 2026.
