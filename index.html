Overview

Keep My Heart SG is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting mental health awareness among children. We believe that every child deserves to have a healthy mind and heart.
Our goals is to provide a inclusive space for children and youth to express their feeling without judgement, and to offer resources and support for those who may be struggling and coping these emotional feelings.
