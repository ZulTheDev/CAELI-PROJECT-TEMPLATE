We Advocate awareness in mental health for children. A safe, fun space where kids express, understand, and grow their emotions

Overview

- Kids (3–14) learn best through fun, short activities; create a safe, calm, and non-judgmental space using simple language and empathy
- Use games and roleplay (e.g. feelings cards) to help kids express emotions and share real-life experiences; cover basics like anxiety, depression, insecurity with age-based activities Teach calming skills and self-understanding; parents should respond calmly and encourage open conversations
- Measure impact with before/after feelings check and feedback; run monthly sessions, ending by June 2025
